Goku

Dragon Ball Z is an anime tv show about a Saiyan named Kakarot(Goku). He was born on Planet Vegeta. This planet was the home of the Saiyans. King Vegeta was the ruler of the planet. Tragedy struck when a villain named Frieza destroys the planet. Goku's father, Bardock, planned ahead of time to put his son in a pod and send him off of the planet to Earth. He was sent there to conquer it but Goku ended up hititng his head due to a crash landing. Despite that, he forgot about it. Martials art master, Master Roshi took him in and trained him to become a warrior. He grew up from a young boy to a strong Martials arts master and overall a nice guy. As strong as he his he's been in numerous fights on Earth against villains. Overtime, all the way up from Dragon Ball to Dragon Ball Super, Goku's managed to go through transformations such as Super Saiyan all the way up to MUI(Mastered Ultra Instinct). No matter how strong he is, Goku still wants to break his limits.
